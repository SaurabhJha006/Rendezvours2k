Rendezvous 2022

AFBBS Annual Inter School Fest




ABOUT US

Warm Greetings! The earnest pursuit of AFBBS is to let this vivacious generation of young enthusiasts to bloom and blossom into well rounded personalities, profound human beings, who are intellectually evolved, yet alert and sensitive towards their surroundings.

‘Rendezvous’ our annual school festival has time and again brought gratifying results in opening up newer and greater prospects encouraging the never-ending spirit of healthy competitions amongst peers. The smouldering days of the pandemic has helped each of us emerge brighter and bolder; it has taught us to be more compassionate, more forgiving, more generous and gentle. In all, we have become better human beings with renewed zeal and zest to savour the treasures of life with a different perspective.

Now, is the time to Rejoice and Reaffirm our Victory. Keeping this in mind, our fraternity invites you cordially to partake in multidisciplinary competitions at Rendezvous’22 on 4th & 5th November 2022. This extravaganza awaits your wholehearted participation in as many events as possible. The multidimensional skills of the young minds of this generation will unfold the festivity through Rendezvous’22.

Please find enclosed the details of various competitions.
We would further request you to register by Friday, 28th Oct 2022.
Let us all Explore, Experiment, Design, Develop and Create together. Let the little sparks catch the fire and produce a Bonfire of memorable moments to cherish eternally.

EVENTS

GUIDELINES

  1. Reporting time is at 0830hrs sharp on both days (i.e.04 Nov & 05 Nov 2022).
  2. The events will commence at 0900hrs in their respective venues.
  3. The participating schools are requested to have a teacher accompanying the students on both days, 4th and 5th of Nov 2022.
  4. Schools competing for the overall trophy to note that points will be awarded for participation in each event.
  5. All related information and updates will be displayed on the school Website: airforcebalbharatischool.com and rendezvous2k.afbbs.co.in
  6. Submissions of the entries should be made as per the directions specified by the Event In-charges.
  7. Submissions must not violate any third-party rights, including, but not limited to, copyrights, trademark rights, or rights of privacy and publicity, contain defamatory statements, include threats to any person, place, business or group or be obscene or indecent.
  8. Decision of the organizers or judges regarding any issue related to RENDEZVOUS 2022 will be FINAL AND BINDING on all schools and participants.
  9. The students of AFBBS will be participating in all the events but will not contest for the overall Trophy.
  10. For any queries kindly contact the respective event in-charges. Contact details are given along with their event on the respective pages.
  11. Result declaration and prize distribution ceremony will take place on 5th Nov 2022 at 12:30 pm.
